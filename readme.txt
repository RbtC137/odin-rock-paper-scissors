1. Understanding
Rock paper scissors is a hand game with two players that compete against each other with showing their moves.
And the moves include three: rock, paper, scissors.
Both player show their move simutaneously and follow the game rule to decide who to win.
The rule is : Rock beats scissors, scissors beats paper and paper beats rock. If both side is the same move, it's a tie.

2. Plan
The game needs player to choose their move as a input, and then generate a random computer move from the three.
Finally judge which side wins.

3.Pseudocode
Player click on a move icon from the page ui.
Generate a computer move randomly.
Compare these moves and output the result.
